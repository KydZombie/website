Card Basics

You can use the buttons below to manipulate the cards.
Press next to view the next card, and show to see its contents.

There's More?

Click the more button to view extra options, such as add and delete.

Making More Cards

Use the add button to add a new card, and delete to remove the currently selected card.

Saving and Loading

The save and load buttons do right what they say on the tin:
Save and load all cards you currently have.

Cleaning Up

The clear button will delete all cards you have currently, but will not get rid of any cards you have saved.
The reset button will get rid of all saved cards.

HTML Goodies!

These cards support HTML tags, such as <b>text</b> for bold and <i>text</i> for italics!
You can get a full list of HTMl tags Here
